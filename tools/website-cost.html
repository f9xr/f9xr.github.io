The Reality of Web Design Pricing

Ever wondered why one agency quotes ₹15,000 for a website while another demands ₹2,00,000? It comes down to value, architecture, and performance.

This calculator doesn't just guess numbers; it leverages aggregated data from top-tier Indian freelancers, boutique studios, and enterprise agencies to give you a transparent look at real-world market rates.

Whether you're a startup budgeting for your MVP or an enterprise planning a major digital transformation, knowing these benchmarks prevents you from overpaying or settling for subpar quality.

Industry Pricing Facts
  • A standard 5-page brochure website built by a professional in India typically costs between ₹12,000 and ₹25,000.
  • Adding E-commerce functionality immediately increases your baseline cost by ₹15,000 to ₹30,000 due to payment gateways and product logic.
  • CMS integration (like WordPress) adds ₹8,000 to ₹18,000 but saves you thousands in future maintenance costs.
  • Requesting a 1-week rush delivery usually forces developers to work overtime, resulting in a 40–60% surcharge.
Pro Budgeting Tips
  • Start with an MVP: Launch your core pages first to test the market, then scale up features using the revenue generated.
  • Don't skimp on design: "Standard" complexity is the minimum for professional businesses; "Premium" is essential if you're building a lifestyle brand.
  • Avoid rush fees: Plan your project 4 to 6 weeks in advance to avoid paying an exorbitant premium for fast delivery.
  • Demand transparency: Always ask your developer for a detailed line-item breakdown of exactly what you're paying for.
Who Is This For?
  • Startups: Estimate your MVP website cost before pitching to investors.
  • Small Businesses: Budget accurately for your professional online presence.
  • Agencies & Freelancers: Provide quick, realistic ballpark estimates to clients or benchmark your own pricing against the market.
