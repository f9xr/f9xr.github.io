Guide

Website Cost Calculator helps you estimate the development cost of a website based on Indian market rates. Enter the number of pages, design complexity, feature requirements, and timeline. The tool calculates a realistic minimum–maximum cost range and breaks down each component. Use it to plan your budget before contacting a development team.

Facts
  • A basic 5-page brochure website in India typically costs ₹12,000–₹25,000.
  • E-commerce functionality adds ₹15,000–₹30,000 depending on product count and payment integration.
  • CMS integration (like WordPress or custom admin panels) adds ₹8,000–₹18,000.
  • Premium design with animations and custom UX can double the per-page cost.
  • Rush delivery (1 week) may increase the total cost by 40–60%.
Tips
  • Start with a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) — launch with core pages and add features later.
  • Choose Standard complexity for most business websites; premium is best for brands.
  • Plan ahead — Standard urgency (4–6 weeks) gives the best value.
  • Always ask for a detailed breakdown when getting quotes from agencies.
Use Cases
  • Startups — estimate MVP website cost before fundraising.
  • Small Businesses — budget for a professional online presence.
  • Agencies — provide quick ballpark estimates to clients.
  • Freelancers — benchmark your pricing against market rates.
  • Students & Researchers — study website pricing trends.
About

This calculator uses realistic Indian market pricing data gathered from freelancers, agencies, and web development firms across India. All figures are in INR (₹) and serve as indicative estimates. Final pricing depends on specific requirements, technology stack, and the development partner. Built with pure JavaScript — no external dependencies for calculations.

Examples
  • Small Business Site: 5 pages, Basic, No E-com, No CMS, Few features, Standard → ₹18,000–₹30,000
  • E-commerce Store: 15 pages, Standard, E-com Yes, CMS Yes, Moderate features, Standard → ₹97,000–₹1,67,000
  • Premium Corporate: 20 pages, Premium, No E-com, CMS Yes, Many features, Fast → ₹2,40,000–₹3,90,000
  • Portfolio Site: 3 pages, Premium, No E-com, No CMS, None, Standard → ₹26,000–₹46,000
