Guide

Local SEO Score Calculator measures your business readiness for local search visibility across 7 key factors. Simply select the options that best describe your current setup, and the tool instantly calculates your score out of 100. Use the personalized recommendations to identify weak spots and improve your local search ranking.

Facts
  • 76% of people who search for a local business visit within 24 hours.
  • Businesses with a claimed GBP see 2.7x more engagement.
  • 64% of consumers have written a Google review for a local business.
  • Mobile-friendly sites rank 5x higher in local pack results.
  • Structured data markup can boost click-through rates by 30%.
Tips
  • Always claim and fully complete your Google Business Profile.
  • Encourage every happy customer to leave a Google review.
  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly — test it with Google's Mobile-Friendly Test.
  • List your business in local directories (Yelp, Bing Places, Apple Maps).
  • Add LocalBusiness schema markup to your website.
Use Cases
  • Local Business Owners — audit your current local SEO readiness.
  • Marketing Agencies — evaluate client profiles before onboarding.
  • Freelancers & Consultants — identify quick wins for your clients.
  • Restaurants, Salons, Clinics — see how you stack up against competitors.
  • E-commerce with Physical Stores — bridge online and offline presence.
About

This tool evaluates local SEO across 7 weighted categories: Website (20pts), Google Business Profile (20pts), Google Reviews (15pts), Mobile Friendliness (15pts), Local Citations (15pts), Schema Markup (10pts), and Social Media Presence (5pts). Built for business owners, marketers, and agencies who want a fast, data-driven local SEO health check.
