Why Hashtags Matter

Hashtags are the keywords of social media. They categorize your content and make it discoverable to users interested in your niche. Posts with relevant hashtags get 12.6% more engagement than those without.

Using the right mix of broad and niche-specific hashtags helps you:

  • Build a community around your content niche

Pro Tips
  • Mix popular + niche: Use 3-5 broad hashtags (e.g., #coding) and 3-5 niche tags (#pythonforbeginners) for maximum impact.
  • Keep it relevant: Every hashtag should directly relate to your content. Irrelevant tags hurt engagement.
  • Don't overdo it: While Instagram allows 30 tags, 5-10 highly relevant hashtags often outperform 30 spammy ones.
  • Refresh regularly: Trends change fast. Update your hashtag sets weekly based on performance data.
  • Analyze competitors: Check what hashtags top accounts in your niche are using and adapt their strategy.
