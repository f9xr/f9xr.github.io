How This Was Calculated

Need help allocating your marketing budget? Contact F9XR Team

Guide

Enter your monthly revenue and select your industry, growth goal, target area, and current marketing channels. The calculator applies industry-standard percentages and multipliers to suggest an optimal marketing budget. It then allocates the budget across key channels — SEO, Social Media, Paid Ads, Content, and Other — giving you a complete picture of where to invest.

Facts
  • Industry base rates reflect typical marketing spend: Services (8%), E-commerce (12%), Food & Hospitality (10%), Healthcare (6%), Education (7%), Real Estate (9%), Tech (15%).
  • Companies targeting aggressive growth typically invest 2.2× the base marketing budget.
  • National campaigns require up to 1.8× more budget than local ones due to broader reach and competition.
  • Businesses with 5+ marketing channels already in place should budget 1.4× more to sustain multi-channel efforts.
  • A healthy marketing-to-revenue ratio for most SMBs is between 5% and 15%.
Tips
  • Start with the calculated budget as a baseline — adjust based on your specific competitive landscape.
  • Track customer acquisition cost (CAC) alongside your budget to measure efficiency.
  • Re-evaluate your budget quarterly as market conditions and business goals evolve.
  • Allocate at least 30% of budget to your highest-performing channel.
  • Always keep a 10-15% reserve for unexpected opportunities or experiments.
Use Cases
  • Startup Planning — Determine initial marketing budget for a new local business launch.
  • Annual Budgeting — Set departmental marketing spend for the fiscal year ahead.
  • Growth Strategy — Calculate the investment needed to achieve specific revenue growth targets.
  • Channel Optimization — Understand how budget should be allocated across channels for maximum ROI.
  • Investor Pitch — Present data-backed marketing projections to stakeholders or investors.
About

This Local Business Marketing Budget Calculator helps small and medium businesses in India estimate their marketing investment based on proven industry benchmarks. Built with pure JavaScript and SVG — no external libraries needed. The allocation model uses standard channel distribution percentages that can be adapted to your specific needs. Ideal for business owners, marketers, and consultants creating data-driven marketing plans.

Examples
  • Example 1: ₹5L/mo revenue, Local Services, Grow 25%, Local, 3-5 channels → ~₹52,800/mo budget (10.6% ratio)
  • Example 2: ₹20L/mo revenue, E-commerce, Aggressive, National, 5+ channels → ~₹5,54,400/mo budget (27.7% ratio)
  • Example 3: ₹1L/mo revenue, Food & Hospitality, Maintain, Local, 1-2 channels → ~₹6,400/mo budget (6.4% ratio)
  • Example 4: ₹50L/mo revenue, Tech, Grow 50%, Regional, 3-5 channels → ~₹12,63,600/mo budget (25.3% ratio)
