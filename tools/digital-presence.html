How to Use This Tool

  3. Follow the Roadmap: Review the personalized checklist below your score to see exactly which missing elements are costing you the most traffic.
The Reality of Digital Presence

Having a website is no longer enough. If your business isn't actively validating its existence across multiple platforms, search engines (and customers) will assume you are closed.

The modern customer journey is fragmented. They might find you on Google Maps, check your Instagram to see if you are active, and read your Yelp reviews before ever clicking the link to your website.

If there is friction or missing information at any point in that chain, you lose the lead.

Presence & Ranking Facts
  • 97% of consumers learn more about a local company online than anywhere else.
  • Businesses that appear on the first page of search results capture 92% of all consumer traffic.
  • A steady stream of online reviews improves your local SEO rankings more effectively than almost any other off-page factor.
Pro Visibility Tips
  • Website First: Your website carries the highest weight (25 points). A fast, modern, custom website makes every other channel perform better.
  • Don't Ghost Social Media: An Instagram account whose last post was from 2019 acts as a negative trust signal. Either keep it active or hide it.
  • N.A.P. Consistency: Ensure your Name, Address, and Phone number are exactly identical across all local directory citations.
Who Is This For?
  • Local Brick & Mortar: Discover why foot traffic is down despite having a good location.
  • Service Businesses: See how you stack up against the competitors who always show up in the "near me" searches.
  • SEO Consultants: Run quick baseline audits for prospective clients to highlight immediate growth opportunities.
About the Algorithm

This calculator uses a weighted point system based on current search engine ranking factors. Core assets like your Website and Google Business Profile carry the heaviest weight, while supplementary signals like YouTube and local citations add incremental trust value.
