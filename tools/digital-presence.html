Guide

Digital Presence Score Calculator measures your business's online visibility across 8 weighted factors: website quality, Google Business Profile, Instagram and Facebook activity, online reviews, content marketing, YouTube presence, and local directory citations. Select the options that best describe your current setup to get an instant score out of 100 with actionable recommendations.

Facts
  • 97% of consumers search online for local businesses before making a purchase.
  • Companies with a strong digital presence see 2.8x more revenue growth.
  • 88% of consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations.
  • Active social media presence boosts brand recall by 80%.
  • Businesses with blogs generate 67% more leads per month than those without.
Tips
  • Start with a professional website — it's the highest-weighted factor (25 pts).
  • Optimize your Google Business Profile with photos, posts, and accurate info.
  • Be consistent across Instagram and Facebook — post at least 3-4 times per week.
  • Encourage every happy customer to leave a review on Google or Yelp.
  • Publish blog content weekly to improve SEO and demonstrate expertise.
Use Cases
  • Small Business Owners — audit your current digital footprint.
  • Marketing Agencies — evaluate client online presence before proposals.
  • Startup Founders — identify gaps in your brand's visibility strategy.
  • Freelancers & Consultants — build a data-driven case for services.
  • E-commerce Brands — strengthen trust signals across every channel.
About

This tool evaluates digital presence across 8 weighted categories totaling 100 points: Website (25pts), Google Business Profile (15pts), Instagram (12pts), Facebook (12pts), Online Reviews (15pts), Blog/Content (12pts), YouTube (6pts), and Local Citations (3pts). Designed for business owners, marketers, and agencies who want a fast, data-driven picture of their online visibility and a clear roadmap for improvement.

Examples
  • Example A: Excellent website, Optimized GBP, Very Active IG+FB, 50+ reviews, Weekly blog, Regular YouTube, 20+ citations → 100/100
  • Example B: Basic website, Claimed GBP, Basic IG, Page Only FB, 1-10 reviews, No blog, No YouTube, 1-5 citations → 27/100
  • Example C: Good website, Optimized GBP, Active IG+FB, 10-50 reviews, Weekly blog, Active YouTube, 5-20 citations → 78/100
  • Example D: No website, Not Claimed GBP, None across social, No reviews, No blog, No YouTube, No citations → 0/100
