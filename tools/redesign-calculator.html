Don't blindly guess your redesign budget. Use this calculator to get a data-driven estimate:

  2. Select Add-ons: Do you need to bolt on a blog, e-commerce, or an integrated booking system during the redesign?

The Reality of Redesigns

A website redesign is rarely just "changing the colors." It is an intricate process of auditing existing traffic, preserving SEO rankings, and migrating databases.

In many cases, a "simple redesign" on a broken, outdated platform actually costs more than tearing it down and building from scratch.

This calculator is designed to expose the hidden costs of redesigning—like CMS migration and SEO retention—so you can approach agencies with realistic expectations and protect yourself from predatory quotes.

Redesign Cost Facts
  • A moderate redesign for a 20-page corporate site in India typically costs between ₹35,000 and ₹70,000.
  • Opting for a Complete Overhaul (new branding, custom animations) will typically increase your base cost by at least 60%.
  • CMS Migration (moving from an old platform to Webflow or WordPress) adds ₹7,000 to ₹20,000 just for the database transfer.
Pro Redesign Tips
  • Protect Your SEO: The biggest risk of a redesign is losing your Google rankings. Ensure your developer implements 301 redirects for every old page.
  • Trim the Fat: If you have 50 pages but only 15 get traffic, don't pay to redesign the dead pages. Prune your site first.
  • Mobile First: Don't just make the desktop site look pretty. Over 60% of traffic is mobile, so demand a mobile-first design approach from your agency.
Who Is This For?
  • Established Businesses: Modernize a legacy 3+ year old site to improve conversion rates.
  • Growing Startups: Budget for scaling a basic MVP site into a massive platform with a CMS.
  • Digital Agencies: Provide clients with an instant, transparent ballpark figure during initial sales calls.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about website redesign costs, timelines, and strategy.

The cost ranges from $3,000 for a basic 5-page redesign to $25,000+ for a 25+ page site with custom features. Factors include page count, design complexity, CMS integration, and additional features like e-commerce or membership systems.
Consider a redesign if: your site is 3+ years old, mobile experience is poor, page load time exceeds 3 seconds, bounce rate is above 70%, conversion rate is declining, or your brand has evolved. A redesign every 2–3 years is recommended.
A typical redesign takes 8–16 weeks: planning and strategy (2–3 weeks), design (3–4 weeks), development (4–6 weeks), testing and launch (2–3 weeks). Timelines depend on project complexity and content readiness.
Choose a redesign if your current platform works well but needs updated visuals and UX. Choose a rebuild if you need a new CMS, major functionality changes, or your current platform is outdated or insecure.
A well-executed redesign can significantly improve SEO by improving site speed, mobile-friendliness, information architecture, and content quality. However, a poorly managed redesign can hurt rankings — always maintain URL structure and redirects.
Businesses typically see 30–50% increase in conversions, 40–60% improvement in page load time, 25% reduction in bounce rate, and 20–30% increase in organic traffic within 6 months of a strategic redesign.
