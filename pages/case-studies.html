Client Overview

SkyLimit Group — Mid-market financial services firm managing 500+ B2B accounts.

Team Size: 45 employees across finance, operations, and management.

Pain Point: AR aging tracked across 12 disparate spreadsheets with no real-time visibility.

Goal: Centralized, automated dashboard with live aging buckets and smart follow-up triggers.

The Challenge

Fragmented Data

SkyLimit Group's accounts receivable team relied on 12+ Excel spreadsheets maintained across different departments. Each sheet had its own formatting, formulas, and update schedule, making it impossible to get an accurate real-time picture of outstanding invoices.

Manual Follow-Ups

Collection follow-ups were done manually via email with no automated triggers. Aging invoices fell through the cracks, leading to a DSO of 58 days — well above the industry benchmark of 35–40 days for their sector.

No Visibility

Management had zero real-time visibility into AR aging buckets (0–30, 31–60, 61–90, 90+ days). Monthly reports arrived too late to take proactive action, resulting in delayed payments and strained client relationships.

Error-Prone Processes

Manual data entry led to frequent errors — duplicate invoices, incorrect aging calculations, and missing payment records. Reconciling accounts at month-end consumed 20+ hours of senior accountant time.

The Solution

F9XR Team designed and built a custom AR Aging Dashboard tailored to SkyLimit Group's workflow. The solution integrated with their existing accounting systems and provided real-time visibility across the entire accounts receivable lifecycle.

Real-Time Dashboard

Centralized AR dashboard with live aging buckets, invoice-level drill-down, and automated data sync from QuickBooks and bank feeds.

Smart Alerts

Automated email and SMS reminders triggered by aging thresholds. Escalation rules ensured overdue accounts reached the right team member instantly.

Trend Analytics

Historical trending of DSO, collection effectiveness, and aging distribution. Management could spot problems before they escalated.

Export & Reporting

One-click PDF and Excel exports with branded templates. Automated weekly reports delivered to stakeholders every Monday morning.

Seamless Integration

Bidirectional sync with QuickBooks Online, Stripe, and bank accounts. Invoices and payments updated in real-time without manual intervention.

Role-Based Access

Custom permissions for accountants, managers, and executives. Sensitive financial data was protected with granular access controls and audit logging.
