Our Process

1. Discovery

We analyze your business needs and define the bot's purpose, features, and user flows.

2. Development

We build your bot using python-telegram-bot or Node.js with clean, scalable architecture.

3. Hosting

We deploy your bot on reliable cloud infrastructure with 24/7 uptime and monitoring.

4. Testing

Thorough testing of all commands, inline keyboards, webhooks, and error scenarios.

5. Monitoring

Real-time performance tracking, error logging, and usage analytics to ensure reliability.

6. Maintenance

Regular updates, feature additions, and Telegram API compatibility maintenance included.
