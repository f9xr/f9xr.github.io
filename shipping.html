By nature of our business, F9XR Team deals exclusively in digital services and intellectual property. This policy outlines how we deliver your project assets.

01

No Physical Shipping

We are a digital-first agency. We do not sell or ship physical products. All deliverables are intangible software assets, design files, or consulting services delivered via the internet.

02

Delivery Methods

Your project assets will be delivered using one of the following secure methods:

Secure Email Transfer
Cloud Drive Folder (Google/OneDrive)
GitHub Repository Transfer
03

Delivery Timelines

Specific delivery timelines are outlined in your customized Service Agreement.

Typical turnaround times:
