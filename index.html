Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of projects do you work on?

I primarily focus on open-source projects in the crypto and blockchain space. This includes dashboards, educational tools, and community platforms. You can see all my public work on my GitHub profile.

Which blockchain are you most excited about?

While I'm interested in the entire ecosystem, I'm particularly excited about advancements in Layer 2 scaling solutions like Optimistic Rollups and ZK-Rollups, as they are crucial for making blockchain technology accessible to a mainstream audience.

How can I start learning about crypto development?

A great starting point is learning Solidity for smart contracts (if you're into Ethereum) and JavaScript for building decentralized applications (dApps) using libraries like Ethers.js or Web3.js. There are many great free resources online like CryptoZombies and the official Ethereum documentation.
