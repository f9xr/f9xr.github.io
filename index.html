How long does it take to build a website?
Standard landing pages take 5-7 business days. Dynamic websites typically take 14-21 days depending on complexity and content requirements.
Do I have to pay for hosting and domain?
Yes, domain and hosting are separate costs (typically ₹3,000 - ₹5,000/year). We assist you in setting up with providers like Hostinger or AWS Lightsail.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
100%. We follow a mobile-first approach. Your site will look perfect on everything from high-end desktops to entry-level smartphones.
