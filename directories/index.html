VETTING GUIDE

Red Flag vs Green Flag

Not every directory is worth your time. Use this comparison to spot quality platforms and avoid SEO-damaging ones.

Red Flags

  • No moderation — spam-filled pages with thousands of junk profiles
  • Instant approval with zero review — signals a link farm, not a community
  • Domain authority below 10 — provides negligible SEO value at best
  • No real users or discussions — dead forums with last post years ago
  • Paid links hidden as "featured" listings with no editorial process
  • HTTPS errors, broken layouts, or aggressive pop-up ads on every page

Green Flags

  • Active moderation with clear community guidelines and visible rules
  • Real user discussions — recent threads, replies, and organic engagement
  • Domain authority 30+ with consistent indexing by search engines
  • Manual review process — profiles are checked before going live
  • Clear link policies — signature rules, post minimums, and anti-spam filters
  • Niche-relevant communities where your target audience actually spends time
