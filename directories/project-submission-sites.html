Frequently Asked Questions.

Project submission sites are online directories, launchpads, and communities where you can list your AI tool, software, or startup project to gain visibility, backlinks, and user traffic. They range from high-authority platforms like DEV Community (DR 90) to niche AI directories.
Each site has its own submission URL. Click the Submit button next to any site to go directly to their submission page. Most require you to create an account or fill out a form with your project name, description, URL, and screenshots.
Domain Rating (DR) is a metric developed by Ahrefs that shows the strength of a website's backlink profile on a scale from 0 to 100. Higher DR sites provide more valuable backlinks for SEO. We recommend starting with DR 50+ sites first.
Focus on directories relevant to your niche (AI, software, startups). Start with high DR sites first for maximum SEO benefit. Quality submissions with good descriptions perform better than mass submissions. Aim for 30-50 quality submissions to start.
Approval times vary by site. Some directories approve instantly, while others may take 1-7 business days. High DR sites often have manual review processes. You can expect initial traffic and backlink signals within 2-4 weeks.
Most directories on our list offer free submissions or a free tier. Some have paid options for featured listings or expedited reviews. We've marked the free and paid options in our directory.
