Project Overview

Client: SkyLimit Group, a 45-person financial services firm specializing in accounts receivable management.

Goal: Replace manual spreadsheet-based AR tracking with a real-time dashboard that provides instant visibility into aging buckets and collection performance.

Audience: Finance team, controllers, and executive leadership who need real-time AR insights for cash flow management.

Stack: React, D3.js, WebSocket for real-time updates, REST API integration with existing ERP system.

The Challenge

Spreadsheet Chaos

The finance team tracked AR aging across multiple Excel spreadsheets, each maintained by different team members. Data was duplicated, formulas broke frequently, and version control was non-existent. Finding the true AR position required hours of reconciliation each week.

Delayed Insights

By the time reports were compiled, the data was already stale. Management made cash flow decisions based on week-old numbers, leading to missed collection windows and extended DSO cycles that hurt working capital.

Manual Labor

Team members spent 15+ hours per week manually updating spreadsheets, sending follow-up reminders, and preparing aging reports for leadership. This time could be better spent on strategic collection activities and client relationships.

No Real-Time Visibility

Leadership had no way to check AR status outside of scheduled reporting cycles. Urgent issues like large overdue accounts or trending delays went unnoticed until the next weekly review, often too late to take corrective action.

The Solution

F9XR Team engineered a custom real-time AR aging dashboard that integrates directly with SkyLimit's existing ERP system. The dashboard provides instant visibility into every aspect of accounts receivable, from high-level aging buckets to individual invoice details.

Real-Time Sync

WebSocket-powered live updates ensure the dashboard always reflects the latest AR data. No manual refreshes, no stale numbers.

Interactive Charts

D3.js-powered visualizations show aging trends, collection rates, and bucket distributions. Drill down from summary to individual invoices with one click.

Smart Filtering

Filter by client, date range, aging bucket, or amount. Save custom views for different team members and reporting needs.

Smart Alerts

Automated notifications for overdue accounts, approaching thresholds, and unusual patterns. Email and in-app alerts keep the team proactive.

One-Click Reports

Generate and export AR aging reports in PDF or Excel format with a single click. Scheduled reports are automatically sent to leadership each week.

Role-Based Access

Granular permissions ensure team members see only the data relevant to their role. Controllers get full access, while collectors see their assigned accounts.
